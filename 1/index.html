This tour is a recipe for a perfect introduction to old
Prague. A mix of the most important pages of history,
details of architecture and stories about modern life
with a pinch of legends and legends. You will pass
through Visegrad and the quays, walk through Prague
castle and reach the old town square, visit St. Vitus
Cathedral and make a wish on the Charles bridge.

What can you expect ?

By bus: from Wenceslas square to the Prague castle
The first part of our busy city trip will take a
comfortable bus. You will pass along the elongated
Wenceslas square, along the Visegrad district with
the ancient fortress, past the Dancing house. Then
"climb" up the hill to the Prague castle (Prague
Kremlin). Of course, the sights will not remain just
points on the map for you — the guide will clearly and
fascinatingly tell you about their history and architecture.
